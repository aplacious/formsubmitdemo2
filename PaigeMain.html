Get To Know Me...

Hi ya'll my name is Paige! I am a photographer based in Rochester, NY but a few times a year I travel to Emerald Isle, NC! I have my bachelors degree in Marketing, but photography is my passion so I am working to become a full time photographer in marketing and portraits. I'm continuing to grow everyday to be the best photographer I can be!


If I could describe my photography in one word I would say "natural". I strive to take the best image I can in order to have the least amount of edits possible, but don't get me wrong I love a good edit! My goal is to show my photography skills more than my editing skills. I enjoy trying to achieve a specific look in my images before the editing process.
