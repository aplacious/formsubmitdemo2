About Me

My name is Paige Mahnke. I grew up in Livonia, NY but currently live in Rochester, NY as a full time student. I am currently working towards my BS in Marketing from the University of Roberts Weslyan. When I am not in school I am capturing the beauty of Rochester. I have been actively taking pictures since I was in high school. Since then I have been my family and friends go to for senior pictures, engagement photos, photoshoots, etc.. Feel free to check out the portfolio I have grown throughout the years and let me know how I can help capture your next event!
